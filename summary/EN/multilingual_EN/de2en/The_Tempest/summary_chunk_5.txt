In search of a villain, Antipholus from Sicily comes to Prospero and demands that he come immediately. When Antipholus from Sicily is no longer with him , he is driven to the island by Sycorax, which Antipholus from Sicily demands from him , which Antipholus from Sicily agrees to tolerate , while Antipholus from Syracuse insists that he should be expelled immediately . As a result , Antiphorio learns from CALIBAN how to curse in his new language and demands that he should act quickly , but if he doesn't act in accordance , he will be crowned as a goddess by his descendants.

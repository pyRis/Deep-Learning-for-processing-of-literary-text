MIRANDA seeks revenge from her father and demands that he abandon the plan. However, he refuses despite his father's pleadings and insists that he abandon the plan. For supper-time, he has to wait for his wife, Miriam, who is holding a book for him, to which he is rejoicing. When he is asked to board a boat, he tells her to do it by mistake. When the boat is empty, the three Sicilian men are joined by pontoon, whereupon they will be able to drink water.

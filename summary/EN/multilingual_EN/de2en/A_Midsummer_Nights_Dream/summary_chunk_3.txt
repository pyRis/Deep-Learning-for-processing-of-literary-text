The two lovers meet in Athens and decide to flee to Hermia's cauldron. When Demetrius appears, he tells them about Hermia's flight and tells them about his love affair with Hermia. Then they proceed to plot against Hermia and Polixenes in a comedy about the murder of Hermia and Polixenes. He orders Leontes to play Thisby's mother and flute. Then he plays the lion and flute's son, Francis Flute , the bellows-mender . At the same time, he plays Robin Starveling, the tailor . At the same time , he plays Tom Snout , the tinker , and Peter Quince .

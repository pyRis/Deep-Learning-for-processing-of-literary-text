Hermia has a secret love affair with Hermia's widow, a dowager of great richesse. Hermione is ambitious and wants to marry the Sicilian lord, but the latter refuses. Then she meets with Hermione and his wife, Helena, in search of a man named Demetrius, whom she suspects is a murderer. The two lovers will meet each other in the city for dinner and dance next to each other, whereupon they will take the flight to Greece, where Hermione and Hermione will be able to find each other. Then they will travel to Greece, where they will meet with Hermione and Demetrius.

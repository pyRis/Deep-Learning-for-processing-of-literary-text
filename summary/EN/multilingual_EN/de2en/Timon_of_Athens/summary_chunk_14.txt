On the way from the palace to Antigonus, Antigonus meets Leontes and his lord Timon, who suspects him of having spent a lot of money on his servants and begs his servants to pay him a little more than he owes. When Antigonus appears, Antigonus demands from his lord that he should pay more than he owes, while Antigonus demands from his servants that they should pay less. At the same time , Polixenes and Hermione are about to be arrested .

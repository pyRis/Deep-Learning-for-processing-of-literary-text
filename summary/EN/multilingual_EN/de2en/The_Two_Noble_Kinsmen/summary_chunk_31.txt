The maid, who is supposed to take care of a hundred black-eyed maids that love as she does , decides to dance with the Duke , while the maid sings: Willow , willow , willow , willow , and between : Prince of Siracusa , fair Palamon , and a tall young man . Bonny Robin asks a tailor to bring the wedding gown that is worn by the bride, but she refuses because she is not a tailor. When the maid asks about it, the tailor replies that no one has lost his maidenhead by cocklight, while the maid insists that she calls the maids and pay the minstrels, which she refuses to do because she has a sister.

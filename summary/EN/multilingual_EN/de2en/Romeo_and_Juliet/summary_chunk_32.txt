The two lovers, friends of Adriana and Polixenes, meet each other in the city for a church service. On the one o'clock the next day, they are celebrating the deflowered Flower of Hath Death with his wife. On the one o'clock before the wedding, Hath Death lain with his wife and his son-in-law. On the one o'clock, he sees the deceased and kills his wife. On the other hand, Polixenes kills his wife and his son-in-law. When First Musician is informed of the death of his wife, he urges his wife to put up the pipes, but she refuses.

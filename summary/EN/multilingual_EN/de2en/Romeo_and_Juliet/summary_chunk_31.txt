On his way back from the vault, he meets his wife Adriana, who is about to marry him. The servants are hungry and want to know if the logs are found. First Servant, a messenger, tells the servants to call Peter. The second Servant, a messenger of witches, tells the servants to search for the logs. The county will arrive shortly afterwards. The child and her mother are abandoned by the shepherd's daughter Juliet, who has just fallen and is now dead.

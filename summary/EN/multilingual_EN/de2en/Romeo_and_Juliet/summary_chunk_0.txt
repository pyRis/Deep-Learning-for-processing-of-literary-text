A dog of the house of Montague moves SAMPSON to the castle, but he is quickly killed by his wife. Along with several of his servants, he is attacked by the raging monkeys and his companions. The raging monkeys are about to take part in a bloody battle against Benvolio and Polixenes, but Polixenes fears for his own life, so he tells his companions to put up his swords and thumbs and enter Citizens with clubs.

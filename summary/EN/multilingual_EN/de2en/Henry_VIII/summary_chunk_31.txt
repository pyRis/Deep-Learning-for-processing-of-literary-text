The queen is labouring with the servants while the queen is being prepared for the labour. The queen and the servants have broken up with the king, but the queen is still in love with him. In search of his wife, he asks her to pray for her immortality, but she refuses. Instead, he orders his servants to enter the house of the Duke of Ephesus and asks his lord the archbishop for mercy, which the archbishop manages to persuade him to grant, despite initial doubts.

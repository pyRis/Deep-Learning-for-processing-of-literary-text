When King Henry VIII demands that he be crowned as the new king, he demands that he must admit all those involved in the affairs immediately. But when he is asked to prove his loyalty to the king, the latter refuses, insisting that he be expelled immediately. When King Henry VIII is speaking to his lords, he has noticed that Tis has a loyal breast. He urges them to do the following: Giving him papers and having breakfast with what appetite they have, followed by saying: Exit KING Henry VIII, who is frowning upon the castle with the nobles and smiling and whispering.

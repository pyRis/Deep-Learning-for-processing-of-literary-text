The Duke of Buckingham comes to court with two Gentlemen to face down the allegations against Cromwell and his wife. The Duke of Suffolk, the second Duke of Norfolk, and the Duke of Hermione, who claims to be high-steward, are also mentioned. Katharine, the princess dowager, is engaged to Kimbolton. The second Gentleman believes that he has just seen a train bearing the sceptre. First Gentleman believes it is the Duke of Suffolk and the Earl of Surrey.

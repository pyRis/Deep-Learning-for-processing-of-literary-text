In order to be married to-morrow, he has to marry the beautiful Lady Ganymede and asks her husband Leonte if he doesn't want to marry the beautiful Lady Rosalind, but she refuses. Tonight, he has to meet the two lovers, who he considers to be married to-bewilder. Tonight, he has to meet the two lovers and sing a song. ToUCHSTONE urges First Page and Audrey to come to hear the song. He urges them to bring in Rosalind.

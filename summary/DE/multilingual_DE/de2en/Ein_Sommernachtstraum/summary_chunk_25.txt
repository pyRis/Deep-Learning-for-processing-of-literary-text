The castle is shaken and destroyed, but the castle itself is reconstructed and renamed. The castle consists of kings, regicided by the Duke of Ephesus, sits at the end of the castle courtyard and dances under the name Hekate. The Duke of Ephesus dances under the name Polixenes and asks all his friends to dance under the name Polixenes, while Polixenes sings in the name of the Duke of Ephesus. The Duke of Ephesus wants to know if his sons and servants are going to behave badly treated if they do not come out of their beds.

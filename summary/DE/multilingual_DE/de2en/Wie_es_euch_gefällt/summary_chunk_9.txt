ADAM discovers his childhood home with the goldsmith's statue in ruins and begs his servants to come and take him to the castle for the murder of the Duke of Ephesus. ADAM and his sons, who grew up with his mother in her mid-twenties-twenties-year-old house with the title of General Hermione, are in search of their descendants. When he discovers them, he orders them to search for their descendants in a forest of trees. When he orders them to search for his ancestors, he orders them to search for them in the forest of drooling trees. When he orders them to search for his ancestors, he announces that he has found them.

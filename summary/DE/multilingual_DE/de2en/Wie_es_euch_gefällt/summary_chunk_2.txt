On his way to the Bohemian palace, he meets his twin sister Rosalinde and Celia, who he mistakes for her brother, he begs them to give him the gold chain because he has just fallen in love with her. He promises to give birth to a child to his wife when he learns that his father has died and is no longer a part of his family. When he promises to be crowned shepherd by his wife, she refuses despite initial doubts.

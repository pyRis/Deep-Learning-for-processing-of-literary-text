Hermione's sons, Bann's descendants, meet Hermione and Amiens in a bear forest in search of goldsmith's works. Hesperia, the widow's daughter of the Duke of Ephesus, discovers that Hermione's child is missing. Hesperia, the widow's daughter, believes that Hermione's childhood friend is in the castle. Hermione urges the Duke of Ephesus to come to the house of the two lovers and bring them home.

The Sicilian kingdom is shaken to the core when he announces that he has just fallen in love with Orlando. On the other hand , however , he manages to get his childhood friend, who was mistaken for his father , to flee to a court of intrigue and revenge. In order to prove his adultery , he orders the Sicilian court , who initially suspects him of lying , to rescue the young man from the situation . In order to prove his adultery , he orders the court , in turn , to sentence the young man , to be crowned .

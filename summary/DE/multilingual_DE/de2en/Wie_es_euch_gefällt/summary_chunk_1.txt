On the way to the new house, ORLANDO meets his father, who promises him that he will get a good life for his daughter, he will be accompanied by his son Adam. On the way, he meets the Duke of Edinburgh with his son Charles, who is leading a rebellion against the new king. On the same day, he also meets the Duke of Scotland with his son Dennis, who is leading a rebellion against the new king. The Duke of Ephesus is visiting the old Herzog in Ardenner Wald. He invites the Bohemian king to dine with his young son Orlando.

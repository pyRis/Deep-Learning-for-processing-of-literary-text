The Duke of Ephesus meets the Duke of Scotland and asks his servants to prepare a coronation for the Sicilian king. The Duke asks the servants to present the goldsmith and the diadem to the Lord Timon, who is supposed to be crowned as the new Generalissimus of Scotland. On the other hand , however , the Duke of Sicily is more surprised , than convinced , by Timon's sudden prophecy , that he will be crowned heir to the throne .

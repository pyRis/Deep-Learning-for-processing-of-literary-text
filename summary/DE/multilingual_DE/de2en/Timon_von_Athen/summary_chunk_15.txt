The Sicilian court orders General Antigonus Thane of Siracusa to be mourned by the Sicilian ruler Adriana in order to be able to prove his loyalty to the Sicilian ruler. As a result, the Sicilian general Bannon is proclaimed the new General of Tu quoque, which ALCIBIADES fears for his position in the war against the Sicilian rebellion, and as a result , he is proclaimed the new General of Desmond. However , he refuses to accept the pronouncements of the new General, which ALCIBIADES fears for his own life .

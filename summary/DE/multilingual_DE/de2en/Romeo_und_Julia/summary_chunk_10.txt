It is the Ostroves and the Ostroves meet up with the sun, whereupon a couple of beautiful sunflowers are delivered to their castle and placed in the centre of the city. Along the way, however, one of the shepherds meets Antipholus of Holde and demands that he tells her about his plan. He refuses to tell her who he is and how he came to the place. When one of the shepherds lands appears, Antipholus of Holde rushes for help.

He is deeply moved in love with his Italian lover, Romeo , but at first refuses to accept his invitation to marry with her. Then he meets Leontes, who is surprised to be engaged to a beautiful Italian girl , who also refuses to marry him . When he sees the beautiful girl in front of his house , he declares that he has just fallen in love with her and promises to get back to her in the end . In order to harmonize with Romeo and Tybalt, he has to harmonize with the Fiedelbogen of his harmonizer.

The Sicilian court is celebrating the witches' victory at a banquet. At the same time , in front of the castle courtyard is a gold chain draped statue of a shepherd holding a monastic statue of Hermione. In response , a courtier appears , accusing the Sicilian king of adultery and revenge . The ghost appears in front of the castle and demands that the abbess be mourned. But the guards be mourned, since the abbess demands that the abbess be mourned and that the abbess be reconciled with the abbess.

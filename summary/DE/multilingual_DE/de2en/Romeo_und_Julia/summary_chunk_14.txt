The Sicilian lord Leontes demands from the Sicilian king that he abandon the plan to marry a Sicilian shepherd and his wife Laura, but the Sicilian lord refuses. Instead, he orders the Sicilian king to tell the Sicilian ruler Peter that he has just fallen in love with his wife. The two lovers, Benvolio and Mercutio, meet up with Polixenes and Peter, who is supposed to be arrested and accused of adultery, in turn prophesy to Polixenes that he will be arrested shortly thereafter.

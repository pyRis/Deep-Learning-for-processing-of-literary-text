After a while, the Duke of Sicily is attacked by a shepherd and killed by a shepherd and his son Antipholus, followed by a thunderstorm of thunderstorms and a thunderstorm of wild noise, followed by a raging thunderstorm of thunderstorms and a thunderstorm of thunderstorms, whereupon the Duke of Sicily is informed that his descendants are about to be crowned as the new King of Sicily. After a while, Polixenes, Stephane, Antipholus and Ariel meet in front of the castle to prophesy that their plan will be executed soon, while the work will be over for some time.

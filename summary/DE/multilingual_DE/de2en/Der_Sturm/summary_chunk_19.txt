Along with Antigonus and Sebastian, Prospero and Miranda, they are attacked by the Sicilian ruler Dromio and Antigonus. Along with the others, they are waiting for the arrival of a messenger with the title of General Leontes, who is supposed to deliver the goldsmith to a shepherd's dinner party. As a result, Antipholus, who is supposed to be crowned as the new General, is informed that Leontes has just fallen in love with him.

The Duke of Hermione leads his servants to Athena's castle and tells them about the splendor of the goddess: Athena, Hermione and Polixenes, kings of Sicily, are in love with each other. The Sicilian king asks the Sicilian ruler Hercules to come to his caesarean section, where he will be crowned as the new King of Sicily, and where he will be crowned as the new King of Bohemia.

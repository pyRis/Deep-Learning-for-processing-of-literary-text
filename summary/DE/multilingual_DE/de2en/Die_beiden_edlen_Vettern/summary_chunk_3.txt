The thought of divine wisdom comes to mind when one sees the thought of confronting the gods with such a brutal weapon as a thunderbolt, when one considers it to be overwhelmed and decides to flee to war against his enemies. However , the gods declare , that nothing can be done about it , and thus , to the dismay of those present , they decide to flee to war against the enemies of Theben and Antigonus.
